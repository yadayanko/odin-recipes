Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is the best chocolate chip cookies recipe ever! No funny ingredients, no chilling time, etc. Just a simple, straightforward, amazingly delicious, doughy yet still fully cooked, chocolate chip cookie that turns out perfect everytime!

Ingredients

  • 227 g salted butter* softened
  • 200 g white (granulated) sugar
  • 220 g light brown sugar packed
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 360 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Â½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt***
  • 350 g chocolate chips (or chunks, or chopped chocolate)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. Set aside.
  4. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  5. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  6. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  7. Add 12 oz package of chocolate chips and mix well.
  8. Roll 2-3 TBS (depending on how large you like your cookies) of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets. (alternately, use a small cookie scoop to make your cookies).
  9. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just BARELY starting to turn brown.
  10. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
